BraydenHepburn GUITARS

Fender and Gibson Guitars are the two most recognizable and influential electric guitar manufacturing companies in the world, and have been for decades. While both so successful, they achieved this while making very different guitars. Their similarites consist of quality in craftsmanship, musical influence, reliablility, distinctive sounds, and prestige, Their differences is what causes many to discuss which one works for them. While one is not better than the other, there are differing characteristics that each brand revolutionized.

Purpose

Here at BraydenGuitars, you can see the differences between the most popular guitars ever made. Gibson and Fender guitar models have dominated the market for over half a century, and this site may help you learn why.

Audience

Our audience ranges from those with any interest to start playing guitar, all the way to the experienced professional. There is always something you can learn with musical instruments, and there is always room to develop more as a listener or player.
